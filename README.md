Fintech Cybersecurity Risk Assessment (GRC Case Study)

Overview

This project presents a structured cybersecurity risk assessment conducted on a simulated mid-sized Nigerian fintech organization handling payment processing and sensitive customer data.
The assessment identifies key risks, evaluates their potential business impact, and recommends practical mitigation strategies aligned with industry-recognized frameworks.

Objective
To demonstrate applied Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) skills through:

Asset identification<>
Threat & vulnerability analysis
Risk scoring (Likelihood × Impact model)
Risk prioritization
Risk treatment planning
Residual risk evaluation

Methodology

Risk scoring model: 1–5 Likelihood × 1–5 Impact
Framework alignment:
ISO 27001 Risk Management Principles
NIST Cybersecurity Framework (Identify Function)

Key Deliverables

Asset Register
Risk Register
Risk Treatment Plan
Executive Summary Report

Key Findings

4 High-Risk vulnerabilities identified
Vendor risk exposure highlighted
Cloud governance gaps discovered
Phishing risk categorized as critical
Strategic remediation reduced overall risk exposure to acceptable thresholds.

Tools Used

Microsoft Excel (Risk Register & Scoring Matrix)
Structured Risk Framework Alignment
Governance-Based Documentation Approach
